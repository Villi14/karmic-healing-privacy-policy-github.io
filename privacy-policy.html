Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Karmic Healing respects your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Data Collection

This app does not collect any personal data. All your reminders, requests, and notes are stored locally on your device. We do not track your usage, collect analytics, or share any information with third parties.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in database system. Your data never leaves your device and is not backed up to any cloud services unless you explicitly choose to do so through your device's backup settings.

Third Parties

This app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any other tracking technologies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store review system or by email.
